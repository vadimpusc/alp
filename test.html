In September 2009, a group of four independent filmmakers from London, UK decided to make a movie. They were never seen again since. This footage found by hotel housekeeping is all that's left of them. In 2011 the police stopped the search. This footage was given to the documentary filmmakers by friends and families in hopes to spread the word and find the missing.
